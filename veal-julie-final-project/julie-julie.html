About Us

JulieJulie ButtonBags

"As the purse is emptied, the heart is filled." -Victor Hugo

JulieJulie was started in 2002 by Julie Lucas Rodenberg, a RCAD graduate with a Graphic Design degree. Being an artist and already set-up at home to do graphic design and monogramming, Julie decided to create her own original bag design and patterns. Julie had three young sons. When she noticed how hard it was to find diaper bags for boys she decided to make her own.
